Adventure Time Character

The Ice King, whose real name is Simon Petrikov, is one of the main antagonists and a tragic figure in Adventure Time. Once a kind and intelligent archaeologist, Simon became the Ice King after he found a cursed crown that granted him powerful ice magic. Over time, the crown drove him mad, erasing his memories and transforming him into a lonely, eccentric villain. He has the ability to summon ice and snow, and often tries to kidnap princesses, believing that they are the key to filling the void of his loneliness.
